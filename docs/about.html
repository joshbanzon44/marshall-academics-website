Project Summary and Details

As our Software Engineering final project, we have developed a portal to simply finding information regarding programs/degress at Marshall University. Marshall's website, while nice, is difficult to navigate and locate information needed on specific colleges or majors. With our academic portal, individuals interested in what Marshall offers can easily discover information they need.

Through the process of the software development life cycle, we have planned, researched, designed, and deployed our system to be readily available to anyone in need. We will continue to update the website each semester as Marshall's cirriculum, website links, and information changes.
